Before joining an engineering college, we all know that participating in the JOSAA counselling process for institutions like NIT, IIT, IIIT, and other GFITs is crucial. Even if you have a good rank, a small mistake during counselling could mean losing a seat. This is where Project Anveshak 1.0.1 comes in—a comprehensive solution designed to make the counselling process easier and less risky.

Key Functionalities

College List

Input Parameters: Preferred college type (NIT/IIT/IIIT/GFTI), category, branch, and All-India rank.
Output: A list of colleges tailored to your criteria to help you decide where to register for counselling.

College Predictor

Function: Predicts the colleges you may get based on your rank, gender, and caste.
Details: Provides comprehensive data, including the probability of securing a particular branch in a specific college.
Note: Predictions are based on historical trends and various assumptions—thus, while accurate, they cannot guarantee a future outcome.

Probable College

Function: Displays a list of likely colleges based on your gender, rank, and caste.
Focus: Only probable colleges are shown, making it easier to focus on realistic options.

College Info

Details Provided: Complete information about the college you select, such as fee structure, rankings, placement packages, etc.
Unique Feature: Directly chat with current college students to get first-hand insights.

Thank you for exploring Project Anveshak 1.0.1. We hope this tool simplifies the counselling process and helps you make informed decisions for your engineering journey!
